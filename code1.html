Cytomegalovirus

Cytomegalovirus

is a double-stranded DNA virus in the same family as herpesvirus found every where,infect people of all ages. In united states 50-80% adults have cmv infection at age of 40. Cmv generally show no sign or symptoms, but being a virus it stays in a person body for life and can reactivate. Infection with the virus can be serious in babies and people with weak immune system . In those with a weakened immune system, CMV can cause serious disease like (retinitis, hepatitis, colitis, pneumonia, or encephalitis). Rarer infection caused by CMV in immunocompetent individuals include Guillain-Barr√© syndrome, meningoencephalitis, pericarditis, myocarditis, thrombocytopenia, and hemolytic anemia.
